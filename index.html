About Me

I started my professional life as an Aviation Electronics Technician for the U.S. Marine Corps. I left the military to persue similar work as a civilian and obtained my Associates in Electrical Engineering. Eventually, I decided while I enjoyed electronics, I wasn't passionate about it. Fast forward a few years and multiple jobs, I found myself succumbing to the idea of working the trade industry. Due to the pandemic, that quickly changed. Sometime during a night of gaming and modding it dawned on me; I have always had a fascination with coding. I spent a couple of months of self-learning, then decided to take a coding bootcamp. From there it's history!
